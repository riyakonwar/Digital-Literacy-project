# [cite_start]Social Media Checklist for College Students [cite: 68]

### ✅ The "Do's"
1. **Check Privacy Settings:** Regularly review who can see your posts on Instagram and Facebook.
2. **Think Before You Post:** Ask if a future employer would be okay seeing this content.
3. **Engage Positively:** Use platforms to share achievements, projects, and networking milestones.
4. **Verify Information:** Fact-check news or campus rumors before hitting the "share" button.
5. **Network Professionally:** Follow industry leaders and alumni on LinkedIn.

### ❌ The "Don'ts"
1. **Overshare Personal Info:** Never post your home address, phone number, or current exact location.
2. **Engage in Trolling:** Avoid heated arguments or using offensive language in comment sections.
3. **Post Without Consent:** Don't share photos of peers or faculty without their permission.
4. **Ignore Security:** Don't use the same password for social media and your college email.
5. **Procrastinate via Scrolling:** Don't let social media usage interfere with academic deadlines.
